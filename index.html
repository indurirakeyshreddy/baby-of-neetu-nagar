About the Name

KLINTARA is an original, Sanskrit-inspired personal name created by our family. It is not a traditional Sanskrit dictionary word, yet it carries a deep sense of spiritual beauty, elegance, and intention.

Sacred Inspiration

KLIM (क्लीं)

A revered bija mantra symbolizing divine love, grace, compassion, attraction, and auspicious blessings. It is traditionally associated with Lord Krishna and Goddess Tripurasundari.

TARA (तारा)

A revered Sanskrit word symbolizing guidance, hope, light, protection, wisdom, and radiance. It is traditionally associated with stars, navigation, and divine illumination.

The Essence

To us, KLINTARA represents a radiant soul blessed with divine grace, wisdom, protection, compassion, inner strength, and the light to inspire others.

Blessing

May KLINTARA be blessed with love, wisdom, courage, good health, prosperity, and a life filled with light, and happiness.
